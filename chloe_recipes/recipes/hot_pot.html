Chloe's Hot Pot

The perfect meal to warm the body and soul. Useful for communal eating as well as dinner for two. Load up the pot with all your favorite meats, veggies, and seasonings to have your very own hot-pot party!

Ingredients

  • Soup base flavoring of choice
  • Noodles of choice(udon, glass, vermicelli)
  • Shabu or Hot Pot Beef
  • Nappa Cabbage or Choice of veggies
  • Miso to enrich soup base
  • Your choice of seasonings

Cooking Directions

  1. Soften noodles in room temperature water.
  2. Start to boil vegetables in water and add broth flavoring. Add water as needed.
  3. Add noodles and let every thing boil together for a few minutes.
  4. Stir, submerge meat until cooked, and add seasonings as needed.
  5. Let cool & enjoy!
