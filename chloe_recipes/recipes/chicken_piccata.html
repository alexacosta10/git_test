Chloe's Chicken Piccata Pasta

A new dish recently added to Chef Cao's arsenal. Perfect balance of creamy noodles and savory chicken. An accessible meal that anyone to can cook and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Noodle of Choice
  • Chicken Breast
  • Heavy Cream
  • Onion
  • Minced garlic
  • Your choice of seasonings

Cooking Directions

  1. Wash & prepare onions, cabbage, & garlic
  2. Season chicken breast then slice into thin cutlets.
  3. Cook chicken and place it to the side.
  4. Cook pasta and place into pan with butter and heavy cream.
  5. Combine chicken in the pan and season. Enjoy!
